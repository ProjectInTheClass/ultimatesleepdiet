Application Definition Statement

An airline application that provides a more convenient travel experience via digitalized boardings and check-ins.

Video

User

  • Persona

    Millenials to Generation Z, who are frequent and/or first time travellers of Asiana Airlines. Users of compatible technological devices and acceptance of online procedures.

  • Addressing the Needs of

    Main use of airline application for E-tickets, to travel lightly. Want easier access and re-view of flight bookings. Wants an in-app flight purchasable atmosphere. Sense of easiness and reliability of the asiana airline application.

  • Design Enhancements

    1. Main function/ task oriented user work flow. Shift from focus of “before” to “while” travellers.
    2.Simplify functions and additional information to relevant and necessary functions.
    3. Provide an informative and organized atmosphere through the use of colour and text size hierarchy. To maintain design consistency.

1. Main Page

Before(left) and After(right), Main page designs of Asiana Airlines Application.

    • The Mainscreen has changed to Find reservation instead of 'Bookings'. This is because most users access the mobile application to view their reservations and Online Check-ins. Thus, for a frequent and already logged in user, Record of booked tickets will be readily available for a fast Check-in & Access to Boarding pass.

    • Once the user has checked in, they will be able to see the changed status of the 'check-in' button and be able to view & use their boarding pass via application.
    Also, the Popover screen can be scrolled down at any other screens by clicking the left arrow button for fold ups&downs.

    2. Book Flights

    • Booking Flights: previous design.

    • Booking Flights: Re design.

    Also, while previous screen had opened a new screen for 'mileage' ticket reservations, Re-designed screen remains in the same page, with added info of possible mileage use.

    • Selecting Flight, Input Passenger Info, Making Payments.
