User

  • Target Users

    Sweet toothed people, desert lovers.
    Mostly Females, college students, who have a rather flexible schedule than other age groups of people.
    Who are on a diet and regularly have a cheat day, but still want to keep track/ be aware of their intake calories.

    • Persona

      Motivation for service: Would like to slack off on a cheat day but still on a diet and want to keep track of calories & status of diet.
      Goals: Work & life Balanced. Seeks healthy lifestyle and body image, but does not repress oneself to an extreme diet.
      Desired Outcomes: Fun cheat days, visual records of cheat days, able to identify patterns and possibly disable future spontaneous cheat days.
      Frustrations: Why do deserts have to be so high calorie?

1. Main Page

    • -->

    Main page for (Existing Users), Users can view the next cheat day and days left till C-day.

    • Users can add their cravings into Wish list for their next C-day. / Input today's consumed calories, if cheated today.

    • In a category of Doughnuts in Krispy Kreme, shows all of their menus with Calorie and Nutritional values. Users can select an item with the portion size they had, which can be selected per 1/4 of a doughnut.

    • After users have inputed the info, they can move to the Cheat today by clicking "sleep it out", which will only appear after an item is selected.

    • In cheat today page, users can see the total calorie consumption converted into hours they need to sleep in order to burn it off. When they decide to sleep, they can click on the "Sleep" which the doughnut reflects their mode of sleep/awake, and when they are awake, they can click back to "awake".

    • Users can also make changes to the items consumed by clicking on it and apply change by clicking on 'sleep it out'.

    • In Cheat Frequency page, users can know their average cheat routine, most favored cheat food and Weekly frequency and monthly planned C-day and Actual C-days.
